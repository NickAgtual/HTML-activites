Lasagna

Recipe Description
This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodle and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil pasta water
  2. Brown the beef
  3. Cook the veggies
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Assemble
